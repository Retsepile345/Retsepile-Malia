Event Schedule

The festival runs for three full days, packed with cultural activities. Visitors can watch live music, traditional dances and storytelling sessions. Workshops for crafts and arts are available for children and adults alike. Each day is designed to give a full experience of Basotho culture.

The programme allows tourists to explore various aspects of the festival at thier own pace. Evening performances feature music and dance competitions. Interactive sessions with artists provide learning opportunities. Overall, the schedule ensures engagement for every visitor.
