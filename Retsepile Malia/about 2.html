History and Significance

The Morija Arts is one of the largest Cultural celebrations in Lesotho. It showcases traditional Basotho art, music, dance, poetry and local crafts.

The event helps preserve Basotho traditions and attracts tourists from across Africa and beyond.

Target Tourists

  • International cultural tourists
  • Students and researchers
  • Art lovers
  • Local families and visitors

Visit Morija and experience Basotho culture.
