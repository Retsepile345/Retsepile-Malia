Welcome to the Festival

The Morija Arts and Cultural Festival celebrates the rich heritage, music, crafts & traditions of Lesotho. Visitors from around the world gather to enjoy performances, exhibitions and the Basotho culture.

Event Details

  • Date: 28-30 september 2026
  • Location: Morija, Lesotho
  • Activities: Music, Art, Traditional Food and Cultural Dances
