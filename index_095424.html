Welcome to the Festival

The Morija Arts and Cultural Festival is an annual celebration that highlights the rich traditions of Lesotho. Visitors from all over come to enjoy music, dance and art exhibitions. The festival creates an opportunity for cultural exchange and promotes Basotho heritage. Tourists can experience traditional cuisine and crafts during the festival.

Each year, the festival attracts artists, musicians, and performers from across the country. It also serves as a platform to educate younger generations about Basotho culture. Visitors can participate in interactive workshops and enjoy traditional storytelling sessions. Overall, the festival is a vibrant celebration of Lesotho's cultural identity.

Event Details

  • Date: 28-30 september 2026
  • Location: Morija, Lesotho
  • Activities: Music, Art, Traditional Food and Cultural Dances
