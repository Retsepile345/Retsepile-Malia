Ticket Pricing

Tickets are available for adults, students and children. Prices are affordable to encourage family attendance. Each ticket grants access to all performances and workshops. Advance booking is recommended as spots fill quickly.

Registration helps us plan seating and manage crowd safely. Attendees can purchase tickets online or at the festival entrance. Group discounts are available for schools and tour groups. The registration process is simple and user-friendly.

  • Adults: M150
  • Students: M80
  • Children: M60
